Membership Application

Rotary Club of Warri Membership Application

To become a member of our Rotary Club, please follow these 3 steps:

STEP 1: Complete and submit the application below.
Note: you are expected to attend at least two club meetings and/or service projects before submitting the Membership Application.

STEP 2: Please contact the President of the Club and Membership chair, Rosana Anchondo at 619-253-4918 to discuss membership requirements including financial, attendance and involvement and to share your interest in joining Rotary. Upon approval by the Membership Committee, your application will be referred to the Board of Directors for final approval.

STEP 3: Once approved, pay your membership dues. You may pay your dues online via PayPal. When payment for dues is received by the club, you will be scheduled for an Induction Ceremony at a club meeting. Dues are not refundable. Please notify the Club at info@philadelphiarotary.org if you prefer to pay by check, and an invoice will be mailed to you.
