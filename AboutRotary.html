ABOUT RCW

Welcome to rotary club of warri

Rotary is a global network made up of neighbours, friends, leaders, and problem-solvers who see a world where people unite and take action to create lasting change – across the globe, in our communities, and ourselves, We currently have up to 58 Members who are eager to solving real problems takes real commitment and vision. Our chhapter started on the 5th Of March, 1979.
For over than 43 years, our members have used their passion, energy, and intelligence to take sustainable projects. From literacy and peace to water and health, we are constantly working to better our world, staying committed to the end.

OUR MISSION

Mission : The mission of Rotary International, a worldwide association of Rotary clubs, is to provide service to others, to promote high ethical standards, and to advance world understanding, goodwill, and peace through its fellowship of business, professional, and community leaders.
"The advancement of international understanding, goodwill, and peace through a world fellowship of business and professional persons united in the ideal of service".

Our Vision

Rotary Vision Statement “Together, we see a world where people unite and take action to create lasting change — across the globe, in our communities, and in ourselves.”
